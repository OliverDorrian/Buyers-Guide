A new era for Cayman real estate

The cryptocurrency market reached a high of US$ 3 Trillion in 2021. Many analysts expect cryptocurrency to eclipse the market capital of gold by the end of the decade, which means over US$ 10 trillion by 2030. To this end, property transactions using cryptocurrency are a game changer for the island’s real estate industry and, ultimately, the economy. It positions Cayman alongside other crypto powerhouses such as Dubai, Switzerland, Germany, the United States, Montenegro, etc. Ushering in a new era with new savvy investors, further bolstering Cayman’s offering over other comparable Caribbean jurisdictions.
